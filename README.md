# LeBron vs. Jordan: An Infographic Comparison

## Overview

The LeBron vs. Jordan debate is one of basketball's most enduring conversations. This infographic moves beyond opinion and legacy arguments to compare their careers through data — championships, scoring, longevity, efficiency, and defensive impact.

**The goal:** Let the numbers inform the debate without oversimplifying two legendary careers.

## Key Comparison Points

| Category | LeBron James | Michael Jordan |
|----------|--------------|----------------|
| Championships | 4 | 6 |
| Finals MVPs | 4 | 6 |
| Regular Season MVPs | 4 | 5 |
| All-Star Selections | 19 | 14 |
| Career Points | 38,000+ (ongoing) | 32,292 |
| Peak Defensive Impact | All-Defensive 6x | Defensive Player of the Year |
| Longevity | 20+ seasons (ongoing) | 15 seasons |

## How It Was Made

1. **Data collection & cleaning (Python)** – Gathered career statistics from multiple sources using Pandas; merged and standardized across eras
2. **Analysis (Jupyter Notebook)** – Calculated per-game averages, playoff splits, and era-adjusted comparisons
3. **Visualization & design (Inkscape)** – Created the final infographic layout, typography, and visual hierarchy
